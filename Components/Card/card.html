Tokyo Olympics 2020

by Mohit Dhatrak

The Tokyo 2020 Games were an unprecedented demonstration of unity and solidarity as the world came together for the first time following the onset of the COVID-19, for an Olympic Games focused on the pure essentials: a celebration of athletes and sport.
